Workflow story

High-risk tasks should pass through visible gates.

Instead of hiding the review model, Axon makes the policy path itself part of the product narrative. That is the whole buying trigger.

01 intake

Read the job with business context already attached.

Tasks arrive tagged by sensitivity, latency tolerance, likely blast radius, and approval posture before any model is selected.

02 route

Choose the cheapest path that still clears the required confidence bar.

The product is not selling “best model.” It is selling an explicit tradeoff engine between cost, speed, and acceptable risk.

03 intervene

Escalate only when the workflow crosses the threshold.

Review becomes targeted instead of universal, which is what makes the software operationally plausible inside a large company.
